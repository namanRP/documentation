## Your Founder Identity

This is about **you**. Your name, role, and a brief bio (think "Ex-Google, Stanford CS") help us personalize introductions and match you with investors who specifically look for founders with your background and expertise.

---

## Your Company's Core Details

Here's where we get to know your business. Provide your company name and website, and our system will intelligently pull and suggest information to save you time. We'll capture your sector, stage, headquarters location, and a clear description of what you do and what makes you unique.

<Tip>This data forms the bedrock of our matching algorithm, connecting you with investors who have a proven interest in companies like yours.</Tip>

---

## Your Fundraising Strategy

This is where strategy meets execution. Tell us about your prior fundraising experience, capital raised to date, and the specifics of your upcoming round.

## Your Business & Traction

Share who your customers are, your business model (B2B/B2C), and your revenue status. Details on key milestones and early traction are invaluable here.

This information allows us to identify investors who invest at your specific maturity level and crafts a stronger, data-informed narrative for your outreach.

<Tip>New milestone hit? Update this section to immediately improve your fit score with top-tier investors.</Tip>
