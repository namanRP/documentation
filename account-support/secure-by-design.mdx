Your fundraising strategy is built on trust. We protect it with a security-first foundation.

## Security that fits your workflow

We provide a streamlined, secure environment for your entire fundraising process. Your data is protected with enterprise-grade infrastructure on trusted, industry-leading servers from AWS, Google Cloud, and Supabase. ## Your data, under your control

You maintain full ownership and control of your information. We operate on a strict need-to-know basis for data processing. Our systems are built to ensure your investor lists, communications, and deal materials are used solely to power your workflow within the platform and nothing else.

## Built with governance in mind

We understand the importance of boundaries and compliance. Our architecture supports strong data isolation and clear access controls, providing a clear audit trail for every action. This disciplined approach gives you the confidence to manage your pipeline, your team's permissions, and your client information effectively, all from one secure dashboard.

## Intelligent security, inside and out

Our AI works for you securely. The platform acts as a responsible gatekeeper, ensuring information is used appropriately to qualify fits and generate insights without overexposure. Security is embedded at every layer, from how we manage access to how we protect your ongoing work.

## A commitment, not a certificate

For our team, security is a core commitment that guides how we build, operate, and evolve. It's woven into our daily practices and our platform's DNA, ensuring we proactively safeguard your trust as part of our service to you.
