## From information to introduction

Here's how you turn an update into a strategic opportunity. We pull the latest public information on firms and people, then map out how you can use it.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Active Investor">

    <Frame>
      **The Intelligence:** Our system shows that Pritzker Private Capital has recently been active in the industrial manufacturing and services sectors, with a stated focus on founder-partnerships and long-term holding periods.
    </Frame>

    #### Your relationship pathway

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Strategic Follow-Up (3–4 weeks later)">

      </Step>
    </Steps>

  <Tab title="Quiet Contact">
    ### Re-engaging a quiet contact (individual-level intelligence)

    <Frame>
      **The Intelligence:** For Adam Ingersoll (Co-Founder/COO, Shasta Dental; Managing Partner, Alair Capital), updates indicate active operational leadership but no major media or new investment announcements in the last 6–12 months. He is scheduled to speak at the 2025 RFK Compass Summer Investors Conference.
    </Frame>

    #### Your relationship pathway

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Pre-Event Value Add">
        Use a scheduled event as a natural, non-intrusive reason to reconnect.

        > *"Adam, I see you're speaking at the RFK Compass Conference this summer. As we build in the adjacent space of [Your Domain], I compiled some data on [Trend Related to His Topic] that might be useful for your panel prep. Happy to send it over."*
      </Step>

      <Step title="Post-Event Insight">
        Follow up by referencing something specific from their appearance.

        > *"Hope the conference was valuable. Your point about [Specific Theme He Mentioned] aligns with a challenge we're solving at [Your Company]. When things settle, I'd appreciate your quick take on our approach."*
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Portfolio News">
    ### Leveraging portfolio news (firm & people intelligence)

    <Frame>

    </Frame>

    #### Your relationship pathway

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Congratulatory & Relevant Outreach">

As a team building in the broader [Legal Tech/Professional Services Tech] ecosystem, we were particularly interested in that move. It signals a focus on [Specific Area]. We're tackling a related challenge around [Your Specific Problem]. Would you be open to a 20-minute intro next month to share what we're seeing?"*

    </Steps>
