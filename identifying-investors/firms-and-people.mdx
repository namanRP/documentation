## A firm is not one entity

A firm isn't a single decision-maker; it's a collection of individuals with different roles, interests, and influence. Success comes from mapping the firm's hierarchy, identifying your champion, and engaging the **right person** with the **right message** at the **right time**.

<Warning>
  You do not want to waste weeks talking to the wrong people—those who don't focus on your stage, sector, or thesis. Every "no" from a bad fit drains your momentum and time. If you don't fix this, you'll run out of runway before you ever reach the investor who was meant to fund you.
</Warning>

## The fundraising operating system

CapitalxAI treats "Firms and People" as the central operating system for your fundraise. It's a dynamic database that helps you discover investors, qualify fit with AI, and intelligently reach out. This is where you move from a scattergun approach to a **strategic pathway**, with all the relationship intelligence and tools you need in one organized workspace.

## Unlock deep intelligence with AI

Next to each investor listing, you'll find the **Analyze with AI** button. Clicking it uses **1 credit** to generate a comprehensive, in-depth profile.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Analyze with AI">
    Select any investor—firm or individual—and click the **Analyze with AI** button next to their listing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI generates a full dossier">
    The system instantly enriches the listing with verified contact details (like direct emails and LinkedIn URLs), detailed investment history, firmographics, and recent news.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on the intelligence">
    A name becomes a full due diligence brief. Use it to personalize your outreach, validate fit, and prepare for your first conversation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Each analysis uses 1 credit. You can always manage your credits and plan on your [Usage page](https://app.capitalxai.com/).
</Info>
