## A list is not a strategy

You have a spreadsheet full of investor names, but no clear plan of attack. Who do you pitch first? Who is a long shot? Figuring this out means hours of cross-referencing their thesis, portfolio, and stage focus against your own company details. This manual process is slow, subjective, and leaves you second-guessing your priorities. You risk starting with mismatched investors, which wastes your most valuable asset: **time**.

## The best investors are a multi-layer match

True fit isn't about one checkbox—it's about alignment across **multiple dimensions**. The ideal investor matches your stage, believes in your thesis, writes checks in your range, and has partners who understand your space. Our AI looks at all these layers at once, giving you a consolidated view of alignment that's impossible to see manually.

<Tip>
  Think of investor qualification like a multi-point inspection. Any single data point can mislead—but when stage, thesis, check size, and portfolio all align, you've found your highest-probability partner.
</Tip>
